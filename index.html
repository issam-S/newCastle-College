Our Mission and Philosophy

Newcastle College is one of the largest education, training and employability organizations in the UK. NCC’s mission is to develop people through learning and achievement, for the benefit of themselves, society and the economy. The focus is on meeting national and regional education, skills and employment goals for individuals and employers in line with government priorities.Our Strategic Plan sets the direction of travel for Newcastle college . It is a statement of our priorities.

The Right Choice for Achieving Your Goals

Newcastle College offers the quality, convenience, and accessibility you need to make your dreams a reality. We’re a small school with a large reach and big impact. Our experienced faculty will guide and inspire you to gain essential skills and knowledge through our rigorous curricula and supportive, interactive classes.The more you learn about Newcastle College, the more you’ll understand what makes us different, and how we can make a difference in your life and your career.

Career Center

Newcastle College offers a wide range of career services to ensure student success throughout their careers. The College works in collaboration with career specialists and faculty advisors to create incomparable professionals those who can really make a difference. Newcastle College specifically caters to working adults who wish to study without compromising on their current jobs. Considering that students at Newcastle College are individuals who seek better employment opportunities, NCC offers them the opportunity to work or intern at reputable organizations. Newcastle College offers focused services to the working professionals who want to pursue higher education. With rich professional experience portfolio and quality education, This is not only a professional’s desire but Newcastle College also sees it as its purpose to get the students prepared for the best so that they won’t have to compromise anymore.
